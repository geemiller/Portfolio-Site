The Temple - 2nd Year Project

Made in Unity Engine, The Temple is the intro to a 3D exploration and puzzle game, with assets created in Blender.

The player is guided through a fantastical forest where they have to use gameplay elements such as exploration and collecting to reach the Temple and eventually solve the puzzle inside of it based on clues they received within the forest.

The Temple was graded with a first-class at university, with >80 for its technical implementation and experience design.

Designed and built by Georgia Miller.
