Harmony Realms - 3rd Year Project

Made in Unity Engine, Harmony Realms is a 3D music theory education game featuring 12 levels - divided into 4 visually and musically distinctive realms.

The player explores the environments to encounter educational learning information in order to solve the auditory musical puzzles by interacting with the various 3D assets.

The 12 auditory musical puzzles were created using digital music notation software Sibelius, using grade 1 theory questions by the ABRSM Royal Schools of Music exam board.

Designed and built by Georgia Miller.
