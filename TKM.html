The King's Meadow - Group Project

The King's Meadow is an immersive branching narrative experience with 200 different passages created with Unity Engine, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in which the user interacts with the story of an ancient kingdom while exploring a 3D whimsical forest.

When the user reaches the end, they are assigned one of eight different flowers that symbolises the choices they made and the journey they took.

The user's result can then be viewed on The King's Meadow website, where they can read further details about their flower, as well as the options to download and share their result.

The King's Meadow was a 2nd year group project where I took on the role of 'Technical Lead' - the project was graded with a first-class at university.
