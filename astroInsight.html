Astro Insight - 3rd Year Project

Astro Insight is an Astrology app made in Android Studio.

Users can: input their birthday and check their daily and weekly horoscopes from an API, receive a personalised tarot card reading based on their location sensor's astro weather data, and be given a palm reading using the device's camera sensor and API.

Astro Insight was graded with a first-class at university.
