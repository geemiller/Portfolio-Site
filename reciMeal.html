ReciMeal - 1st Year Project

ReciMeal is a prototype for a mobile app created in Adobe XD that makes a personalised dinner recipe for the user each day, dependent on the data that is inputted through 5 option sliders with categories such as 'hunger level' and 'tiredness'.

The recipes are all entirely created by the ingredients that the user already has in their own kitchen, with a built-in AR scanner that scans in each of the user's ingredients along with their use-by date and quantity.

ReciMeal was graded with a first-class at university, with 90/100 for its design quality, and 80/100 for its technical quality.
