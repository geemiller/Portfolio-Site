Temporal Shift - Masters Group Project

Made in Unity Engine, Temporal Shift is a 3D Puzzle Platformer revolving around shifting time and flipping gravity.

You play as Lyra, an agent working as a Temporal Shifter, who experiences a failure in her ship's Hyperspeed, causing her to crash on an unknown planetoid. Explore this strange galaxy to repair your broken ship in this realm of ever-changing time shifts.

Temporal Shift was a University group project where I took on the role of programmer.
