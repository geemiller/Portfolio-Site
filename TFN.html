The Forgotten Nexus - 3rd Year Project

Made in Unity Engine, The Forgotten Nexus is a scene from a 3D narrative and puzzle game, with models and assets created in Blender.

The player is guided through an otherworldly space while on a journey to save a futuristic city from darkness, they have to complete 3 different puzzles in order to collect electricity capsules to give to the Nexus to help recharge the city.
